Drug interaction: warfarin + aspirin
Increased bleeding risk due to combined anticoagulant/antiplatelet effect. Monitor INR closely.

Drug interaction: lisinopril + potassium supplements
Risk of hyperkalemia. Monitor serum potassium.

Drug interaction: simvastatin + clarithromycin
Increased simvastatin levels raising myopathy/rhabdomyolysis risk. Avoid combination.

Drug interaction: metformin + contrast dye
Risk of lactic acidosis; hold metformin around contrast procedures per protocol.

Drug interaction: sildenafil + nitrates
Severe hypotension risk. Contraindicated combination.
